How payments work

Secure online payments, settled to your bank

For platforms that collect payments, Arnex uses Xendit, a licensed payment gateway, to process transactions securely. We never store your customers’ card details.

Customer pays

A member or customer pays online with GCash, an e-wallet, a card, or bank transfer.

Processed securely

Xendit handles the transaction on a PCI-DSS-compliant platform. Card data never touches our servers.

Reflected instantly

The payment is recorded and reflected on the customer’s account inside the platform.

Settled to you

Collected funds are settled to the organization’s registered bank account.

Payment methods and settlement are provided through Xendit. Availability of specific methods depends on the platform and the customer’s region.
