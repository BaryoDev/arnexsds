Services

What Arnex builds and runs

Software, delivered end to end — and, where you need it, the secure online payments that go with it.

Custom software development

Web applications, APIs, dashboards, and internal tools built specifically around your organization’s processes. We handle design, development, and deployment as one accountable team.

Online payments & collections

Accept payments online through GCash, e-wallets, cards, and bank transfer via our payment partner Xendit. Payments are recorded and reflected on the payer’s account automatically, and collected funds are settled to your organization’s bank account.

Events & registrations

Publish an event — a fun run, seminar, or fundraiser — with online registration and payment. Attendees sign up and pay in one step, and you get a live list of who has registered and paid. Launching soon.

Legacy modernization

Carry an aging system forward without a risky rewrite. We modernize incrementally, keeping the software in production and the business running the whole time.

Systems integration & APIs

Make your existing tools work together — accounting, payments, messaging, and third-party services — through clean, documented, well-tested APIs.

Cloud, deployment & DevOps

Hosting, containers, TLS certificates, automated backups, and deployment pipelines, so your software stays online, secure, and recoverable.

Maintenance & support

Retainers for ongoing updates, monitoring, and support after launch. We keep the software healthy so you can focus on running your organization.

Subscriptions & platforms

Ready-made platforms such as BaryoClub (membership & treasury) and a full-suite accounting module, available on monthly or annual subscription. See Products.

Engagement & pricing

How we work with you, and how you pay

We keep pricing transparent and agree on it before any work begins.

Project-based

For custom software and modernization. We scope the work and agree on a fixed quote or milestone payments up front. Invoices are issued and paid securely online.

Subscription

For platforms and add-ons such as BaryoClub and accounting. Billed monthly or annually, cancellable in line with our Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Payment collection

For platforms that collect payments from your members or customers. Funds are processed by Xendit and settled to your organization’s bank account, net of applicable fees.

A note on payment security. Card and e-wallet transactions are processed by Xendit, a licensed and PCI-DSS-compliant payment gateway. Arnex does not collect or store your customers’ full card details on its own servers.
